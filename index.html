About Pedda Harivanam

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Pedda Harivanam village is 594067. Pedda Harivanam village is located in Adoni mandal of Kurnool district in Andhra Pradesh, India. It is situated 20km away from sub-district headquarter Adoni (tehsildar office) and 120km away from district headquarter Kurnool. As per 2009 stats, Pedda Harivanam village is also a gram panchayat.
